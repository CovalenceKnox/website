Bio

Covalence is an alt metal/post grunge band from Knoxville, TN, USA. With influences such as Tool, Deftones, and Pearl Jam, their music blends hard hitting guitar riffs with dreamy and dark atmosphere. They have released one EP, Alpha Decay, and are currently working on a full length album.
